How does Pets To You work?

First things first you select an animal you like, we have all sorts of pets to choose from. We have a cats, dogs, reptiles, birds, cows, horses, and fish. Once you choose which one you want you put in payment information and an address for delivery. In a few days' time, your brand-new pet will be delivered straight to your doorstep.
